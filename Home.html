What's the Big Deal About Climate Change?

It's more than just hot days! It’s about our weather going wild, sea levels rising on our coasts, and making it harder for farmers to grow our food. It's caused by 'greenhouse gases' – like CO2 from cars and factories – trapping heat like a blanket around the Earth. Understanding it is the first step to fixing it!
