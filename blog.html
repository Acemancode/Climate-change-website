Ghana's Climate Vibe: What’s REALLY Going Down?

Wassup, GreenVibers! Think climate change is some faraway issue? Nah. It’s happening right here, right now in Ghana. From crazy weather to changes at the coast, it's real. But guess what? So is the action! Let's check out what’s been making waves...

Our Coastline is Feeling the Heat! 🌊

You know Keta and Ada? Places our parents and grandparents chilled? Well, rising sea levels are a real threat. But good news! Projects like WACA ResIP 2 are pumping millions into protecting these areas. And shouts to groups like Green Growth Africa planting mangroves in Kedzi – these trees are like natural bodyguards for our coast!

May 2025

Galamsey vs. Our Water - It's Serious 💧

Real talk: Illegal mining (Galamsey) is messing up our rivers big time. Think Pra, Ankobra, Birim – getting choked with chemicals. Experts even warn we might have to *import* water by 2030 if this continues! Protecting our water sources isn't just a vibe, it's survival.

April 2025

Power Up! Ghana's Solar Flex 💪☀️

Forget 'dumsor', let's talk 'sun-sor'! Ghana is seriously boosting its solar power game. We added tons in 2024, and big companies like MTN are going solar too. Banks are even helping people get loans for panels. More sun power = less pollution. It's a win-win!

May 2025

Youth Dem Making Moves! ✊🏾🇬🇭

Don't let anyone tell you young people aren't doing anything! The KMA just launched another round of its Youth Climate Action Fund for folks (15-24) in Kumasi with bright ideas. And top dogs at the EPA are urging us to get clued up. Our voices (and actions) matter!
