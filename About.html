About Us

GreenVibe is a youth-led environmental initiative dedicated to raising awareness, promoting and leading climate action in Ghana. We believe in the urgent need to address climate change.

Our mission is to empower the next generation. GreenVibe brings together passionate young individuals, schools, and volunteers to lead impactful projects - from tree planting drives to community cleanups and educational workshops. We aim to equip the next generation to take ownership of their environment and create a more sustainable future for all.

Our Accomplishments

Educated over 100,000 students on climate change and sustainable practices across Ghana.

Planted over 50,000 trees in collaboration with local communities and schools.

Partnered with government and NGOs to advocate for stronger climate policies.
